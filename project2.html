Project365

enhanced reading experience

Overview

With the fast-paced lifestyle, we often forget how important it is to read. One should read every day not only to gain knowledge but also to improve the focus and concentration. The Project365 allows the user to read and discover the short and easy to read articles published in various categories. The aim of this project is to inculcate daily reading habit among users by providing delightful reading experience. This was a side project of my developer friend Saurabh Mhatre. I collaborated with him to handle all the design requirements as a UX Designer.

My Role

UX Designer

Tools used

Adobe Experience Design, Adobe Photoshop

Objective

Encourage users to read something daily, by creating engaging reading experience.






DISCOVER

User Research

In this project, due to time constraint, I decided to conduct a quick online survey asking users about their reading preferences. Getting to know about reading habits of the users was the most important part. Based on the information collected, I was able to derive following assumptions about target user.

  • Not enough time to sit and read.
  • Unable to find articles related to their interests.
  • Discovers interesting articles but unable keep list of them.
  • Articles containing advertisements and breaks hampers the reading experience.
  • Wants to share articles with their friends and colleagues.
  • Most of the users prefer to read articles on their smartphones.







EXPLORE

Initial Wireframes

While deciding the task flow and features of the project, it was crucial to first think from different perspectives and explore multiple design solutions. As a part of ideation, I drew wireframes to try various navigation patterns and ways to represent articles in card format.







DEFINE

Final Solution

After evaluating each option thoroughly, I decided to go with the optimal design solution. I had my interface clearly defined before I moved to the visual design part.


The high-fidelity Mockups represent the final design solution. Dark theme is used for enhanced reading experience. With the inclusion of bottom navigation, app’s important sections can be navigated easily. The user can explore different categories in discover section. Each article can be saved and accessed later from the saved section of the app. The user can mark topics as favorites and based on the preference, feed of articles from those topics will be provided.










Things learned

I started working on this project when it was just a simple idea. I learned how to define the requirements clearly which guided me in the right direction during the entire design process.

empathizing with your user by understanding their goals and motivation really helps while taking important design decisions.

In the next phase, user onboarding process can be included so that new users will get familiar with the app and its features. Push notifications and a functionality to keep track of user’s reading activity can really help this project meet its primary goal.
