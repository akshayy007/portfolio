Moplex

video streaming platform

Overview

Moplex is an online video streaming platform just like Netflix but with many different types of content ranging from movies, TV shows, music videos to short videos. This was a personal project, a design challenge that consisted of creating online video watching experience for the diverse set of users. It was a challenging task to deliver the consistent experience which involved different types of video content. The objective was to formulate an engaging and intuitive online video watching experience that enables users to enjoy their favorite content seamlessly. DISCOVER

User Research

To understand the end user better, I conducted 5 user interviews. Here the main goal was to understand users habits and their pain points. I also observed how users were interacting with their favorite apps to study their behavior. It provided the valuable insights to build the user personas of the target audience.

some of the insights were:

  • Most people prefer to watch short videos.
  • Average time spent on online video watching was around 40-45 min.
  • They do not want to miss out on latest content.
  • Sometimes not enough bandwidth to enjoy uninterrupted videos.
  • Unable to discover regional content in their preferred language.
  • Worried about exhausting mobile data.


User Persona

Based on the information collected from user research, I was able to create some user personas representing target users.






EXPLORE

Initial Wireframes

User research helped me to gain the deeper understanding of the problem that needed to be addressed in the final design solution. To explore the options, I sketched low-fidelity wireframes to try out various possibilities and use cases.






DEFINE

Final Mockups

After exploring various options and deciding the best optimal solution, the final output consisted of high-fidelity UI mockups. In this design, different types of video content is represented by different sizes of artwork. It follows simple navigation pattern of scrollable tabs which allows user to browse main sections of the app by using simple swipe gesture, reducing the overall cognitive load.
