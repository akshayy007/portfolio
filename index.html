About Me

I am a masters student studying Human-Computer Interaction at Indiana University. I have 4+ years of experience in designing interactions for consumer and enterprise products.

As a UX/Product designer, I am driven by my passion to design the new and redesign the old. Possessing an engineer’s meticulousness with an artist’s creative mindset, I constantly strive to create beautiful, novel designs that are intuitive as well as engaging. I love to challenge myself, and always try to keep up with the latest and greatest in the world of design and technology.
